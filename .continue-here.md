<decisions_made>

- **Cinematic modules chosen**: Curtain Reveal + Text Mask Reveal + Kinetic Marquee — selected for brand fit (bold, dark, cinematic, field-tough). Text mask was user's favorite.
- **Three text mask sections**: "NO BULLSHIT" → "STRENGTH" → "FREEDOM" — builds Foreman AI's full tagline across scroll depth

- **Video layer deferred**: Structured the video `<video>` element in HTML with commented-out `<source>` — ready to activate when real job site footage is available
- **No shadows rule enforced**: Brand kit specifies border + background contrast only, no `box-shadow` anywhere in the page
- **Three pillars confirmed from docs**: Voice Agents (Retell.ai/GPT-4o/ElevenLabs), Workflow Automation (n8n), Permit & Ops — based on reading ~/docs/retell-agents-summary.md and ~/docs/plans/2026-02-03-foreman-ai-crm-design.md
